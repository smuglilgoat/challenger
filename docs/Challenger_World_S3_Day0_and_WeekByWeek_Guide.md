# MapleStory Challenger World Season 3 — Complete Day 0 + Week‑by‑Week Progression Guide

A consolidated, step‑by‑step plan built from the four source guides (Jenna's Day 0 checklist, Kobe's Complete Document, the two‑creator "Ultimate Progression Guide," and the written Season 3 Progression doc).

> **Read this first.** This is the *optimal / sweaty* path. It assumes you are **maining** your Challenger World character on **Challenger Heroic**, and that you buy the core passes (Challenger's Pass + Genesis/Jenny Pass, and a Shine/Goddess Pass if you play Aerolia/CSO). If you're free‑to‑play, building a mule, or waiting on Kinesis, skip to **Section 9 — Branches**. None of this is required to enjoy the season — take what's useful and don't burn yourself out. Numbers come from a pre‑patch test server, so treat exact figures as estimates until GMS patch notes are final.

## 1. The big picture (what you're aiming at)

- **Challenger World 3 is only ~12 weeks long** (GMS shortened it). That short window is the single most important planning fact — it's why Hard Kai by **week 3** matters for getting two Eternals, and why you progress bosses aggressively instead of farming in place.
- **Item Burning runs ~21 weeks** and the **Equipment Rentals last until Nov 10, 2026** — both outlive Challenger World itself, so your burning gear and rentals carry you the whole way.
- **Day 0** is the few hours *after the game comes up but before weekly reset* (maintenance and reset are both Wednesday). If the game launches in time, weeklies and event‑shop stock reset right before the weekly reset — letting you claim some things twice. That's the entire point of "sweating" Day 0.
- **Event reset is Tuesday** (Western hemisphere). **Boss reset is Wednesday.** These are two separate deadlines every week — plan accordingly.

### Boss tier list (rough damage gates)

1. **Free bosses** — Hilla, Pink Bean, Zakum, Pierre, Bonbon, Von Leon, Magnus, Vellum, Papulatus, Normal Lotus, Normal Damien. Nuked with item burning + free boss accessories. Can one-shot most of them within the first couple hours.
2. **Small step** — Normal Slime, Easy Lucid, Easy Will. Incredibly easy after hitting 6th job, even with very early 6th-job skills.
3. **Still easy** — Normal Lucid, Normal Will, Normal Gloom, Normal Darknell. Need more Arcane Force for later bosses. Still pushovers at Lv 260+ with early 6th job.
4. **First hard bosses** — Hard Lotus, Hard Damien, Normal Verus Hilla. Lv 266+ recommended. Need some fragment masteries unlocked. Should have 10k challenger points by now for the special skill ring (Roar or Continuous).
5. **The gauntlet** — Chaos Slime, Hard Lucid, Hard Will, Chaos Gloom, Hard Verus Hilla, Hard Darknell, **Normal Kai**. Lv 270+, rentals help a lot. Massive set of bosses. Need class knowledge. Defeating all gets you to Diamond tier.
6. **Early Grandis** — Normal Seren, Easy Kalos, Easy Adversary. Lv 275+ recommended. Far more complex mechanics than previous bosses. Get knowledge up before or while fighting them.
7. **Finale** — Hard Black Mage, Hard Seren, Hard Kai. Lv 280+ strongly recommended. Hard BM can technically be done in tier 6 but takes ~1 hour; at tier 7 it's ~30–35 minutes. Hard Kai requires level 280 minimum.

- **SAVE your Sol Erda / "order" request this week. Do NOT complete it.** It's once per account — if you finish it now, you can't claim it on Day 0. Claiming it the moment you hit 260 before weekly reset is free progress (90–180 frags).
- **Save your MVP weekly perks** (Forex coupons, etc.) so you can claim them on Day 0 if the game comes up in time.
- **Preload NX / Maple Points** — roughly **300k NX / ~$300** covers the passes below. Jenna's total Day 0 spend: ~$110 for Shine Pass + Challenger's Pass + Journey Pass.
- **Pick your IGN.** If you want a specific name held by an existing character, delete that character **24 hours before** launch (must be in ghost form with the 24‑hr cooldown expired), then be ready to delete the placeholder again at launch.
- **Pre‑download the client on BOTH Steam and the Nexon launcher.** Race them at launch and kill whichever is slower — this is your best shot at logging in immediately. (Don't forget the download itself eats into your Day‑0 window.)

With basic gear + free cubes from the event, they can clear Normal Lotus & Damien easily. These 3 mules generate ~4.2 bil/week in boss income — huge for Heroic where mesos are scarce. Save a character slot for the Kinesis hyperburn in July.

## 3. DAY 0 — the full sequence

This is the intense part. Plan for **~3 hours of game uptime before reset** (minus download time). One creator timed a similar run at ~2h46m (skipping 6th job but spending time on drop rate). Have the checklist on a second screen, game full‑screen, minimal distractions. Jenna plays with game sound only, one monitor for checklist, one for game, laser focused.

### A. Get in and make the character
1. Win the download race; log in the instant the game is up. Start both Steam and Nexon Launcher downloads simultaneously, kill whichever is slower.
2. **Pick the correct world: Challenger Heroic.** There will be **two** heroic worlds — do **not** pick Interactive. People brick themselves here every season by loading up on Interactive when they actually want Heroic. There is only ONE Challenger Heroic world to pick.
3. Create the character. On the creation popup, **select the MIDDLE option** (not the others — the wrong one costs you the name or requires a relog). You can make another character but you'll lose the name.
4. **Designate as a Hyper / Beyond Burning character** before hitting 260, and type **"beyond burning"** exactly in the box (nothing else).
5. Skip the tutorial cut‑scenes (the old 6th‑job‑skip bug from PTS was fixed) and get to **Lv 30**. Just go for it, skip everything.

### B. Power to 200 and set the foundation
6. Accept the **Challenger World event** and claim the **creation reward** (top‑right of the Challenger World UI).
7. Equip the **Maple Pearl set**. Use **Tera Blink to 200** (~10 min of dialogue/menu‑gaming; level skills as you go to minmax/optimize).
8. Get **5th job** (very fast now — it blinks you around to all NPCs).
9. Claim **Hyperburn rewards** — but **do NOT use the Eternal Flame title or ring yet** (save them; title order matters — see §8). You CAN claim the Infinite Flame coupon from the hyperburning UI, just don't USE it from your inventory.
10. Buy the **Shine/Goddess Pass** premium (~35k Maple Points). Redeem the box for the **EXP title** + **Pennant of the Spirit**; equip and copy the pennant straight away. Put **EXP into hyper stats**.
11. Redeem **20 node stones → max Holy Symbol** (helps Strawberry Farm EXP).
12. Start **Professions + Auto Harvest immediately** (it counts down ~2.5k hits even offline; the sooner it's running the sooner you get juice and can start making laps). Start this ASAP so every day you can run the full 4 hours.
13. Redeem **Challenger's Pass** for the mob‑damage bonus (+200% normal monster damage — nice for Monster Park Extreme and future training).
14. Open **one Arcane/Mystic Seal** — just open one, get whatever you get. If it's rare, just put it on. It's a little bit of extra damage for free.

### C. The leveling box sequence (→ 260, then → ~266–268)
Use the Shine/Goddess Pass + Summer Countdown reward boxes. **Use untradeable vouchers first** so you can save tradeable strawberry/EXP/magpots for a future mule or Kinesis.

**Strawberry tickets first:** 2 strawberry tickets from the Lv 200 box → ~215. (The Lv 200 box also gives 3 magpots you'll use later.)

**Then ~13.7k EXP vouchers, 215 → 250:**
- 1,000 from Power of Time (top‑left)
- 1,000 from the Lv 210 box → 220
- 1,000 from the Lv 220 box → 225
- 5,000 from the Lv 225 box → 235
- 2,000 from the Lv 230 box + 5,000 from Power of Fate (top‑middle) → 240
- 3,000 from the Lv 240 box + 10,000 from the bottom Lv 240 box → 250

**250 → 260:**
- *Claim but don't yet use* the 5,000 from the Lv 250 box.
- Use **1 magpot** (of 3 from the Lv 200 box) + **1 magpot** from Summer Countdown → 255.
- Use the remaining 5,000 vouchers → **260**.

**260 → ~266–268** (after setup; uses remaining EXP virtues + the **10k Advanced tickets**):
- 1,000 (Lv 220 box remaining), 3,000 (Lv 240 box remaining), 10,000 EXP virtues (paid Shine/Goddess box), 5,000 (Lv 250 box), and the big **10k Advanced** box → **266**, then dailies/weeklies push to **268**.

> **XP‑timing tip (from the Ultimate guide):** save the **10,000 Advanced EXP vouchers** to push **to exactly 270**, not 268. Express boosters are tuned for **270–280**, so reaching 270 first makes every later booster more efficient and unlocks Angler. You'll land ~272 by end of Day 0's training and ~275 by event reset (day 7).

> **Alternative path for Aerolia with Shine Pass:** Use the 10,000 advanced vouchers from the Shine Pass to get to ~264 at 25%. Then use 60–70 express boosters from the Burning Express Pass + 2,000 advanced vouchers from the Express Pass. That's enough for ~3 levels (264→270). This is the Aerolia-specific fast track.

### D. Get the Genie weapon + Genie Pass
15. Grab the **Genie weapon**, redeem the **Genie/Genesis Pass**, and **proc it now** (it makes killing C10‑tier bosses a little easier). Remember the Genie Pass is **30k NX, not Maple Points.** The Genesis Pass also gives you a boss buff every time you fight any boss that gives traces (+20 ATK, +20 all stat, +10% boss, +10% IED, +1000 HP, +150 Arcane Force). You do NOT get this buff on Slime or early Grandis bosses — only on Black Mage commanders and above.

### E. Character setup
16. Start the **Jenny (liberation) quest**. **Skip the Cernium story.** Don't rush 6th job yet (unless you want easier item‑burning bosses — fine to do before or after, 6th job unlock is much faster this patch).
17. **Fuse all Arcane symbols** (visit every town to unlock them all first). Between hyperburn (~Lv 7) and the pass (~Lv 10), fusing gets you ~Lv 11–13. **Fuse the Cernium symbol**. Use the **trait boost potion on Charm** (Charm gives final damage via Insight profession).
18. Equip the **Boss Accessory box** (~9-piece set, pre-cubed), the **Abyss Hunter ring**, and **one Event Ring** (all event rings are identical — just pick any). In the pendant selector, **pick DOM (Dominator) over Abso** — even though you get two selectors, just get the Dominator from both. Put on the **Hyperburn outfit**.
19. **Unlock all symbols** — visit every town. Dailies/weeklies push symbols to ~Lv 15 by week 1.
20. **Start every event:** Challenger Partner, Arcane Seal, Beyond Burning, Item Burning, Knight of Fantasms (Phantasms), Burning Express, Auto Harvest. **Activate the passes** you bought (Challenger Pass, Burning Express, Genesis Pass, Challenger EXP Duo if applicable).

### F. Claim and distribute event rewards
21. Claim the rest of **Summer Countdown** + **Shine/Goddess Pass**:
    - **Honor (80)** → use on your character for inner ability rolls.
    - **20 EXP nodes** → max V Matrix (100 EXP nodes = 300 regular nodes in the new system).
    - **Symbol selectors** → distribute evenly for **max Arcane Force**.
    - **90 frags** → use on your character.
    - **10 boosters** → save for training.
    - **Unique scroll** → save for secondary or other gear.
    - **10 cubes (3×)** → hold for now.
    - **Event ring** → claim the one you don't have yet (all three are identical).
    - **Legendary ring scroll** → use on an event ring.
    - **100 event cubes, 50 black flames, 20 bright cubes** → **save** for now.
    - **30 Selector Symbol Vouchers** (free pass): each gives 5 Arcana + 2 Grandis symbols.
    - **200 Selective Symbol Vouchers** (paid pass).
    - **300 EXP nodes** (paid pass).
    - **5 hours VIP sauna** (paid pass).
    - **100 Special Medal of Honors** (paid pass).
    - **400 fragments** (paid pass).
    - **300 black flames** (paid pass).
    - **40 bright cubes** (paid pass, heroic-only count).
    - **2,000 advanced EXP tickets** (paid pass).
    - **1 transcendent growth potion** (paid pass).
22. **Skill keybinds, hyper skills, hyper stats, pets.** For the **vac pet**, the min‑max move is to **save it** until you have meso/drop gear (no point looting before then). Join your class Discord if you haven't — learn your rotation.
23. **V Matrix:** max **common nodes, skill nodes (trios), Holy Symbol, Goddess**, and common skills (Weapon Aura, Mana Overload). Leave utility skills — **Speed Infusion, Combat Orders, Advanced Blessing, Sharp Eyes, Rope Lift — at level 1** (they don't improve with levels). You get a ton of the new V‑matrix points from the passes, so this is fast.

### G. Item burning + the Day‑0 money run
24. Put on **item burning gear**. **Prog item burning through Normal Lotus & Damien.** Then do **all daily/weekly bosses**.
25. **Day‑0 only:** **sell all your crystals** now (you'll get a fresh batch at reset, so double‑dipping is free). *If there's no Day 0, don't sell — you'll get better crystals later in the week.* Crystal management is critical: you can only sell **180 crystals/week** account-wide. If you boss on multiple characters, sell the highest-value crystals first.
26. **Push item burning to clear Easy Lucid** (this awards the **18‑star gear** with improved potentials: two prime unique lines instead of prime+regular, e.g. 9%/9% instead of 9%/6%). Path: Chaos Zakum → Vellum → Lotus → Lucid. You can do all of this in **practice mode** (full buffs). The Burning Boss Accessory box (~9-piece set, pre‑cubed) + the free **Dominator** make Easy Lucid an easy clear. You also get **Ignitia keys** (2 per completed mission; all 4 from the first four missions) — these let you swap a piece to better gear without losing set effect. They work on anything except Challenger/Eternal/Sengoku gear.
27. **Money run** (longest part, ~30–45 min): clear everything you can. The Ascent skill makes most content super easy. Expect ~**1.7 bil** (if Aerolia bug fixes are fixed, it should be light work). Spend it immediately on the Day‑0 gap items from the **Meso Shop → Special tab**:
    - **2 Mecha Berry tickets** (these reset on **weekly reset**, not event reset — buy now, buy again at reset).
    - **Express boosters** — 10 for 300m, 2 for 1 bil. *Save ~1 bil of mecha berries for the Lv 280 power spike;* they scale up to the 290s (giving ~3T at 280, up to ~6T) and last until ~September.
    - **Seal (ceiling) wands** — ~150m for 30, for the Arcane Seal.
    - **Optional lower spend:** At 1.45 bil you can get 4 Mecha Berry + 5 Express boosters. This means you don't need to kill Easy Lucid and Normal Slime — just do Damien and below + Vellum/Hilla and you should have enough.
28. **Auto‑clear Epic Dungeon / High Mountain** (saves ~30–60 min on low spec; gives ~40 frags + ~0.5 Sol Erda + ~13% EXP).
29. **Monster Park** (hard nerfed, ~5 min solo, juiced by the Challenger Pass +200% mob damage — they changed the XP so it's much better at later levels) and **Monster Park Extreme** (~14% EXP, not bad to solo — they may have made it easier this patch).
30. **Use the 10 express boosters while getting the 5k‑kill weekly** (~17 min — boosters last ~100s each, ~36% EXP). Best map is the highest you can access (~Lv 269 mobs). The map layout has changed — looks like Night Road maps in the big boy areas. Place your summon/spear/shower for max efficiency. Do the **Hotel Arcus daily** (this is the one daily you do manually on Day 0 — everything else is skipped via passes), then **7 Monster Park runs** for the final bump.
31. End Day 0 around **~268** (or 270 if you saved the Advanced vouchers per the XP tip). With all dailies completed, you'll have enough to hit 268. This doesn't include XP from training with boosters.

### H. Day‑0 cubing & scrolls
32. **Event ring selectors (≈4):** from Summer Countdown, the Lv 210 box selector, the internal/4th unique coupon — **pick different rings each time** so you collect all three + the **Challenger‑exclusive ring** (heroic‑only). You get another coupon later, so make sure to pick different ones.
33. **Legendary scrolls (3):** one from Summer Countdown, one from the Lv 260 Shine box (usable on anything — ideally a **ring**, not an emblem), and one from Winter Survival (if that event ships). Then use **solid cubes** to roll the ring for **item drop**.
34. Day 0, **use as few cubes as possible** — once you hit an item‑drop line, run with it for bossing. Item drop is only really useful for Princess No on Day 0.
35. **Winter Survival event** (if available): similar to the Ren patch event from CW2, now re-released for the Crown patch on KMS. Includes another ring selector, a 30-day vac spirit, more cubes, and a unique emblem if you kill Vellum. Rewards include crazy amounts of symbols. Complete this for extra damage and the unique emblem.

## 4. The daily routine (every day, all season)

Burn this into muscle memory:

- **Event check‑ins**, then put **event buffs into Monster Park XP** (Sacred Power and Arcane Force are the next best).
- **Daily bosses** + **symbol dailies** + **Ursus** (~120m/day) + **Monster Park** (7 runs).
- **5k‑mob research**, then **Challenger World check‑in** (1k) + **Burning Express check‑in** (1k).
- **Make another Lv 200 alt via Tera Blink** to use your *extra* Monster Park runs (you get 14/day = two sets of 7) — slowly levels free mules. Every character created on Challenge World is a terror burning character.
- **Prog item burning / Winter Survival** mission (at least to Easy Lucid practice mode).
- **Follow the XP plan** toward 270 → 275 → 280.
- **Manage crystals:** you can only sell **180 crystals/week** account‑wide. If you boss on multiple characters, **sell the highest‑value crystals first** — don't brain‑dead sell everything.
- **Arcane Seal:** never cap over 20 seals (you get 3/day, even offline). Buy wands the day before shop reset. Use expiring wands. If you get Legendary, stop saving — open for essence to tier up.

### Weekly housekeeping (two reset deadlines)
- **Before EVENT reset (Tuesday, Western hemisphere):** claim all **Challenger Pass mission XP** + the **10k‑mob research** mission.

## 5. Week‑by‑week plan

> CW3 is ~12 weeks. Weeks 1–7 are the structured climb; weeks 8–12 are "keep soloing the hardest boss you can, party higher, and grind Eternals/pitched." Levels assume the passes + ~2 hrs/day farming (you'll likely beat these if you farm more).

### Week 1 — Establish (target Lv 270, ideally 275)

**Bossing progression (do in this order):**
1. Solo **all free bosses** (Hilla through Normal Damien) — nuked with item burning.
2. Solo **Normal Slime, Easy Lucid, Easy Will** — easy after 6th job.
3. Solo **Normal Lucid, Normal Will, Normal Gloom, Normal Darknell** — need more Arcane Force.
4. Solo **Hard Lotus, Hard Damien, Normal Verus Hilla** — Lv 266+ recommended.
5. Solo **Chaos Tenebris and below** — the main week 1 bossing goal.
6. **Try Normal Seren solo** — clearing it Week 1 fast‑tracks item burning massively. Most people can't yet; if not, **party Hard Seren / Easy Kalos**.

**Rank climb as you clear (claim rewards at each step):**
- Clear Normal Lucid → **Silver** (10k points): claim frags + **OZ ring** (free ring!), upgrade Hexa.
- Clear Hard Lotus/Damien + Hard Will → **Gold** (15k points): claim frags, upgrade Hexa.
- Clear Chaos Gloom / Hard Lucid / Chaos Slime → **Platinum** (20k points): claim 300 frags + 5 Sol Erda, upgrade Hexa.
- **At Lv 271, clear Normal Kai → Diamond** (40k points): claim legendary pot scroll + 60 bright cubes + cozy winter set.

**At Lv 270, rent gear** (everything except the OZ ring, which is free from Silver rank). Save **~2 bil** to clear Chaos Tenebris (you need it for the rental purchase). Rentals last 2 days now (up from 1), so rent near end of week for maximum value. All accessories including totems = ~1.6 bil. Available until Nov 10, 2026.

**Character setup this week:**
- **Inner ability:** Use 10 Circs from Hyperburn + Honor to roll your first good line.
- **Emblem:** Claim from Winter Survival mission (unique emblem) if available. Otherwise use what you have.
- **Secondary:** Use unique scroll on Frozen Secondary. Just try to get any single useful line (attack% or boss%).
- **Event rings:** Claim the other 2 event rings from Summer Countdown and Winter Survival. Use event ring legendary scroll on one. Use the many event ring cubes to try to tier up ALL event rings to legendary (this is good tech — bright cubes are expensive this early).
- **Remaining event rings:** try to get 4-line meso on them.
- **Hexa:** Get **Janus to Lv 10 first** so grinding isn't miserable, then follow your class Discord's Hexa order.
- **Professions:** Use profession max potion from Shine/Goddess Pass to max **Insight** (gives final damage).
- **Arcane Seal:** Open just for a rare one and settle. Don't chase higher tiers yet.
- **Beyond Burning:** Start it now.
- **Symbols:** Combine Lv 5 Cernium symbol from Shine Pass with Lv 5 from hyperburning. Save SAC symbols for Arcus/Odium. Combine Lv 10 symbols from Shine Pass with Lv 10 from hyperburning. Distribute Arcane Force selectors evenly.
- **Inventory cleanup:** Use transparency slots from Winter Survival if available.

**Items:** roll **5‑line meso, 4‑line drop** across 9 accessories first; *then* go for **hybrid / double‑drop** lines. Always keep **~1.4 bil** banked for next week's rentals.

## 8. Title order (don't overlap them)

1. **Eternal Flame** — from your first Arcane Symbol after 5th job. Use it until 270. *Do not auto‑claim Infinite Flame early.* You can claim the coupon from the hyperburning UI, just don't USE it from your inventory.
2. **Beyond Burning** — after Lv 270 + finishing the Beyond Burning event. Same stats as Infinite Flame **but +100 SAC (vs 50) + 100 Arcane Force** — huge for early Grandis. Use this immediately at 270. Lasts ~2 months.
3. **Infinite Flame** — claim **only after Beyond Burning expires** (~2 months). By then your SAC makes the 50‑SAC difference irrelevant, giving you ~4 more months of a strong title.

**Total title coverage: ~6 months of strong titles if you don't overlap them.**

## 10. Events quick‑reference

### Arcane / Mystic Seal
- 3 control rods/day (even offline), **hard cap 20** (you stop earning at 20 — never cap out).
- Buy 30 wands/week (~5m each) from the in‑UI meso shop; they last 7 days, so buy them **late in the week** and save them until your rank gate lets you push for **Legendary**.
- Don't chase Legendary before **Stage 4–5** (rates double from 4→5).
- Goal: hit Legendary **once**, then dismantle everything else for upgrade mats.
- Settle points: Stage 1 → Rare +6, Stage 2 → Epic +6, Stage 3 → Unique +6, Stage 4 → Unique/Legendary, Stage 5 → Legendary.
- A **+7 of one tier = +1 of the next tier.**
- All seals at same upgrade level have identical stats (only differs by class).
- Meso shop sells 30 wands for 5m each weekly.

### Challenger Partner
- Pick carefully (you're **locked in by contract** — cannot change partner).
- Duo (practice mode OK):
  - Hard Will → 100 frags
  - Hard Verus Hilla → 100 frags
  - Easy Adversary → partner ring (only displays in towns, both must wear it, stand next to each other)
  - Hard Seren → partneroid (android that takes partner's appearance at time of clear)
  - Normal Kalos → soul partner medal (shows partner's name at time of clear)
- Hunting EXP is intentionally mediocre (16% at 260, 7% at 270, 1.7% at 280 per full week of 100k mobs). Don't sweat it or rage at your partner.

### Challenger EXP Duo
- 10,000 NX/week for a ticket
- Put on main-world character, then kill mobs on Challenger World
- First 200k mobs: 4 points each (most efficient)
- Next 80k: 3 points, next 95k: 2 points, rest: 1 point
- Caps at 645k mobs/week for 1.5M points
- At Lv 291, capping gives ~5.3%/week (requires ~31 hours/week)
- 200k mobs (~10 hrs/week) is more realistic and still good value

### Coin shop
- The Challenger Shop sells **600 Sol Erda Fragments** (500 via regular coins at 40 coins each, 100 via Advanced coins from Normal Kai+).
- Buy 3x weekly from boss coin shop and Challenger shop.

### Trace Restore / pitched (long‑term)
- Run Trace Restore missions weekly (practice mode OK — points **carry to your main world**).
- Cap **12 Star Spec boxes/day**.
- Loot **Pitched Whisper Crystals** and save **390** for a Genesis Badge before spending.
- With booming‑free 22★, **9‑set is realistically reachable**; the 10th piece is refreshing Black Hearts or a Total Control drop.
- Don't use Trace Restore missions early — they transfer after Challenger World ends.
